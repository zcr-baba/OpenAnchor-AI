### Executive Summary
OpenAnchor is an open crowdsourcing-driven AI poisoning governance & content authenticity verification platform. We harness community power to label toxic data sources, block misinformation, and build a trustworthy AI ecosystem for LLM vendors and end users.

### 项目简介
OpenAnchor（开放锚）是一款开放众包驱动的AI投毒治理与内容真实性校验平台。我们汇聚全民力量标注AI投毒污染源，阻断虚假信息传播，为大模型厂商与终端用户共建可信AI生态。

## 📢 Project Background | 项目背景

### English
The 2026 CCTV 3·15 Gala exposed a critical industry risk: **AI data poisoning has formed a complete black industry chain**. Malicious actors use GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) technology to mass-produce fake promotional articles, flood the entire web, and manipulate mainstream LLMs to package unqualified products and false information into "standard answers". This severely misleads consumers, undermines market fairness, and erodes public trust in AI technology.

Current industry solutions have core shortcomings:
- Pure technology-driven verification engines always lag behind the adversarial iteration of the black industry, and cannot cover new toxic sources in real time.
- Lack of a governance system with public participation, making it difficult for a single platform to fight against large-scale false information flooding across the web.
- General fact-checking tools cannot specifically identify the typical characteristics and communication links of AI poisoning.

**OpenAnchor was born for this — driven by the dual wheels of "crowdsourced labeling + intelligent verification engine", to fight against the AI poisoning black industry from the root and guard the truth of information in the AI era.**

### 中文
2026年央视3·15晚会重磅曝光行业核心风险：**AI投毒已形成完整黑色产业链**。黑产通过GEO（生成引擎优化）技术批量生成虚假软文，集中投喂全网，操控主流大模型将三无产品、虚假信息包装成“标准答案”，严重误导消费者、破坏市场公平、瓦解AI技术公信力。

当前行业解决方案存在核心短板：
- 纯技术驱动的校验引擎，永远滞后于黑产的对抗性迭代，无法实时覆盖新增毒源。
- 缺乏全民参与的共治体系，单平台难以对抗全网规模化的虚假信息投喂。
- 通用事实校验工具，无法针对性识别AI投毒的典型特征与传播链路。

**OpenAnchor 应运而生——以「全民众包标注+智能校验引擎」双轮驱动，从根源上对抗AI投毒黑产，守护AI时代的信息真相。**

## 🎯 Core Positioning | 核心定位

### English
OpenAnchor is the world's first **open crowdsourced authenticity verification infrastructure focusing on AI poisoning governance**, providing full-link capabilities for three core user groups:
1.  **To C End Users**: Zero-threshold AI content authenticity verification tool, one-click identification of false information and poisoning traces, to keep users away from AI consumption traps.
2.  **To B LLM Vendors/Developers**: Out-of-the-box API/SDK for toxic source feature library, pre-block poisoning data in the reasoning link, and improve the credibility of model output.
3.  **Community Contributors**: Open toxic source labeling platform, everyone can participate in labeling false information and pollution sources, to jointly build the world's largest AI poisoning governance knowledge base.

### 中文
OpenAnchor 是全球首个**聚焦AI投毒治理的开放众包型真实性校验基础设施**，面向三类核心用户提供全链路能力：
1.  **To C 终端用户**：零门槛的AI内容真实性校验工具，一键识别虚假信息、投毒痕迹，远离AI消费陷阱。
2.  **To B 大模型厂商/开发者**：开箱即用的毒源特征库API/SDK，在推理环节前置阻断投毒数据，提升模型输出可信度。
3.  **社区贡献者**：开放的毒源标注平台，人人可参与标注虚假信息、污染源，共建全球最大的AI投毒治理知识库。

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Open Crowdsourced Toxic Source Labeling System | 开放众包毒源标注体系

- Zero-threshold participation for all: Minimalist operation interface, one-click labeling of AI poisoning sources, fake products, and misleading information.
- Distributed review mechanism: Multi-round cross-verification + authoritative review to ensure the accuracy and timeliness of labeled data.
- Contribution incentive system: Record the labeling achievements of each contributor, and build an open and co-governed trusted community.
- Real-time updated global AI toxic source feature library: Gather labeled data from the whole network, synchronize new poisoning features in minutes, to counter the rapid iteration of the black industry.

#### 中文
- 全民零门槛参与：极简操作界面，一键标注AI投毒污染源、虚假产品、误导性信息。
- 分布式审核机制：多轮交叉核验+权威复核，确保标注数据的准确性与时效性。
- 贡献激励体系：记录每一位贡献者的标注成果，共建开放共治的可信社区。
- 实时更新的全球AI毒源特征库：汇聚全网标注数据，分钟级同步新增投毒特征，对抗黑产快速迭代。

### 🛡️ Enterprise-Grade Authenticity Verification Engine | 企业级真实性校验引擎
#### English
- Multi-dimensional verification capability: Four-in-one system of "toxic source feature matching + multi-source cross-verification + semantic consistency check + common sense compliance detection", to accurately identify AI poisoning traces and false information.
- Full-link credibility scoring: Output 0-100 credibility rating for AI-generated content, mark risk points, trace information sources, and provide interpretable verification reports.
- High performance and low latency: Distributed architecture design, supports millisecond-level verification response, and adapts to high-concurrency enterprise-level scenarios.
- Special optimization for vertical fields: Customize exclusive verification rules and knowledge bases for high-risk poisoning scenarios such as e-commerce, medical care, and finance.

#### 中文
- 多维度校验能力：**毒源特征匹配+多源交叉验证+语义一致性校验+常识合规检测**四维一体，精准识别AI投毒痕迹与虚假信息。
- 全链路可信度评分：为AI生成内容输出0-100分可信度评级，标注风险点、溯源信息来源，提供可解释的校验报告。
- 高性能低延迟：分布式架构设计，支持毫秒级校验响应，适配高并发企业级场景。
- 垂直领域专项优化：针对电商、医疗、金融等投毒高危场景，定制专属校验规则与知识库。
